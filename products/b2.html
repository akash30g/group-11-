Dalmatian Sailfin Molly Male


 

Price - $17

Origin: Mollys are a standout amongst the most vivid, and most well known aquarium fish.Their predecessors initially lived in seaside territories of southern Mexico in freshwater, harsh water, and saltwater. Yet, Mollys are presently discovered living in aquariums all through the vast majority of the world.

Behaviors: Most Mollys are very active swimmers, and chase each other, but are rarely too aggressive. 
Life Span: Mollys live for a few years. In the meantime the next generation of Mollys have usually been born. 

Feeding: Premium Fish Food Flakes when small and Premium Pellet Food when larger.
