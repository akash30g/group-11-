Guppy


 

Price - $11

Origin: Guppies are one of the most colorful and most popular aquarium fish. Their ancestors originally lived in Northeastern South America, but Guppies are now found throughout most of the world. There are many color varieties for sale in this store.

Behaviors: Most male Guppies eat, chase female Guppies, and quarrel with other male Guppies. Guppies are not aggresssive to other fish. 
Life Span: Guppies live for a few years. In the meantime the next generation of Guppies have usually been born.

Feeding: Premium Fish Food Flakes.
